New Ideas (Discovered)

Thermoelectric Generators in Waste Heat Recovery:
New materials and designs for thermoelectric generators can convert waste heat from industrial processes, cars, or even household appliances into usable electricity, thus reducing energy waste.
Artificial Photosynthesis:
This mimics the natural process of photosynthesis to convert sunlight, water, and CO2 into fuel like hydrogen or other hydrocarbons.It provides a sustainable way to produce fuel while also reducing CO2 from the atmosphere, helping to combat climate change.
Carbon Capture and Storage(CCS)
CCS captures carbon dioxide from power plants or industrial sources and stores it underground or uses it in other processes.This helps reduce greenhouse gas emissions from existing fossil fuel-based infrastructure while new renewable technologies scale up.

Magneto-Biochemical Energy Harvesting

Develop a system that leverages the magnetic properties of certain biological molecules, such as hemoglobin in blood, to generate energy. By using magnetic fields to induce controlled biochemical reactions, electrical energy could be harvested from natural processes occurring within living organisms or synthesized bio-mimetic structures

Photosynthetic Artificial Intelligence (PAI) Panels

Build structures inside coastal caverns that use the natural oscillations and resonance of ocean tides to generate power. The generators would harness acoustic and hydraulic pressure from the tidal movements to drive turbines or piezoelectric elements embedded in the cavern walls.
