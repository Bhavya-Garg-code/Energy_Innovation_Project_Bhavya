Energy

Energy is the capacity to perform work, driving physical processes and changes in systems. It can neither be created nor destroyed, only transformed, and is fundamental to all forms of activity and existence.

Why To save energy?

Environmental Protection:

  • Reduces carbon footprint and greenhouse gas emissions.
  • Decreases pollution and helps mitigate climate change.

Economic Benefits:

  • Using less energy reduces monthly utility costs.
  • Saving energy diminishes the need for importing expensive foreign oil and gas.

Social Benefits:

  • Reduced pollution from lower energy consumption leads to fewer health issues like asthma and respiratory diseases.
  • Lower energy demand reduces dependence on foreign energy sources, enhancing national security.

Technological Advancement:

  • The demand for energy-efficient technologies drives innovation
  • Energy efficiency creates jobs in manufacturing, installation, and maintenance of efficient systems.
